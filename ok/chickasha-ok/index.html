Chickasha, OK Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Chickasha /ˈtʃɪkəʃeɪ/ is a city in and the county seat of Grady County, Oklahoma, United States. The population was 16,051 at the 2020 census, a 0.1% increase from 2010. Chickasha is home to the University of Science and Arts of Oklahoma. The city is named for and strongly connected to Native American heritage, as 'Chickasha' (Chikashsha) is the Choctaw word for Chickasaw.
